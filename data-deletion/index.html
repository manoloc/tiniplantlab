Data Deletion

Instructions for removing Tiny Plant Lab data.

Local game dataAccount dataSupport dataContact

Tiny Plant Lab does not currently require or create a player account. Standard game progress is stored locally on your device.

Delete local game data

You can remove local progress by using an in-app reset option when available, clearing Tiny Plant Lab’s app storage in your device settings, or uninstalling the app.

On iPhone or iPad

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Go to General → iPhone Storage.
  3. Select Tiny Plant Lab.
  4. Choose Delete App. Offloading may preserve app data, so use Delete App if you want the local data removed.

On Android

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Go to Apps → Tiny Plant Lab → Storage.
  3. Select Clear storage or Clear data, or uninstall the app.

Warning: deletion is permanent unless your device platform has separately preserved a backup.

Account and cloud data

There is currently no Tiny Plant Lab account or Tiny Plant Lab cloud-save database. Therefore, there is normally no server-side player account for us to delete. If accounts or cloud saves are added later, this page and the Privacy Policy will be updated with an online deletion process.

Support correspondence

If you previously contacted us and want your support correspondence deleted, email privacy@tinyplantlab.com using the same email address you used to contact us. We may retain limited records where required for security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or legal compliance.

Third-party data

Apple, Google, analytics providers, and advertising providers may separately process store, device, analytics, payment, or advertising data. Requests concerning their records should be made through their privacy tools and account settings.

Contact

If these instructions do not solve your request, contact privacy@tinyplantlab.com.
