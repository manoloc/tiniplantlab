Frequently asked questions

Where is my progress saved?

The current version stores your garden progress locally on your device. It does not currently use an account or cloud save.

Can I move my garden to another phone?

Automatic transfer is not currently available. Platform backups may help in some cases, but they are not guaranteed. Cloud saves may be considered for a future update.

Why did my plant grow slowly?

Check its preferred moisture and mineral ranges, current weather, and soil type. Conditions outside its preferred range can increase growth time or reduce its harvest.

How do I get more seeds?

Harvest produce, then grind it in the Mill. You can also purchase basic seeds in the Market and discover new ones through Laboratory experiments.

How do hybrid discoveries work?

Combine two seeds in the Laboratory. Known recipes show possible outcomes, while repeated experiments gradually reveal hidden lineages.

Does the game require an internet connection?

Core local gameplay is intended to work without a constant connection. Store services, analytics, advertisements, sharing, or future online features may require internet access.

How do I reset my garden?

Use the reset or new-game option in Settings or Progress when available. You can also clear app storage through your device settings, but this permanently removes local progress.

Troubleshooting

  1. Close and reopen Tiny Plant Lab.
  2. Confirm your device has free storage and an up-to-date operating system.
  3. Restart your device.
  4. Install the latest game update from the official store.
  5. Before reinstalling, remember that local progress may be lost.

Privacy and data requests

Tiny Plant Lab does not currently create player accounts. Most gameplay data remains on your device. Read the Privacy Policy or visit Data Deletion for instructions.
